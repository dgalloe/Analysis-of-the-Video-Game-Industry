# Analysis-of-the-Video-Game-Industry
Analyzed global video game sales to inform strategic store decisions. Examined platform lifecycles, genre trends, and regional preferences to optimize purchasing and maximize revenue. Provided data-driven insights for market success.

- Analyze historical video game sales data to identify market trends
- Determine the lifespan and profitability cycles of gaming platforms
- Identify top-performing platforms, genres, and regions for sales optimization
- Build user profiles and preferences across key gaming markets (NA, EU, JP)
- Test statistical hypotheses regarding user review impact on sales performance

## 2. Key Findings üèÜ
- Identified PS2, X360, PS3, Wii, and DS as historically dominant platforms
- Determined typical platform lifespan averages 5-8 years before decline
- Found action, sports, and shooter genres consistently drive highest sales
- Discovered significant regional preference differences:
- North America/Europe: Shooter and sports genres dominate
- Japan: Role-playing games show strongest performance
- Established ESRB rating preferences vary significantly by region
- Demonstrated statistical evidence supporting correlation between critic reviews and sales
